mashed potatoes

DESCRIPTION

The aroma of mashed potatoes will always remind me of dinner at grandma and grandpa's house!

nutrition

226 calories; protein 6g; carbohydrates 36g; fat 7g; cholesterol 15.9mg; sodium 42.9mg

ingredients

  • 3 Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and chopped
  • ⅓ cup milk
  • ¼ cup sour cream
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

steps

  1. Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, about 20 minutes. Drain.
  2. Mash potatoes with milk, sour cream, salt, and pepper in the large pot.
